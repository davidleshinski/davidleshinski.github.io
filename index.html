A little about me...

I am currently a Master's student pursuing an M.S. in Data Analysis and Visualization at the City University of New York Graduate Center (CUNY Graduate Center). During my studies, I will be learning not only how to collect and analyze data, but also ways creatively visualize the data using different software and coding languages. With this knowledge, I will be able to create data visualizations that are informative and interactive for others who are trying to understand the data.

Months before I enrolled in the Data Analysis and Visualization program, I graduated from the State University of New York at Cortland (SUNY Cortland). There, I earned a bachelor's degree in Communication Studies with a concentration in Public Relations and Advertising.

As an active student amongst the Cortland community, I joined the Dragon Chronicle, a student-run newspaper, where I was a Staff Writer. My duties ensured that I was up to date in current news locally and nationally and that I kept our readers updated as well by writing weekly articles for publication.

While studying at Cortland, I was able to complete an internship at Pinckney Hugo Group (pinckneyhugo.com), a full-service marketing communications firm. As a Public relations intern, I was able to gain hands-on experience in a team-based environment. With no two days being the same, I was able to complete tasks ranging from writing press releases on new hires and building contact lists on Excel to searching for errors in a newly built website and coming up with new keywords on Google Ads.

Samples of my coursework, internship work, and independent work can be found in the "Work Samples" section here. My resume is available here. Feel free to look around!
